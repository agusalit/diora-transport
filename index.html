Tour Ideas

Plan Your Adventure

Explore Bali's most iconic destinations with us to get the best experience and recommendation

Uluwatu - Jimbaran - Tanah Lot

Sunset Temple Tour

Visit three of Bali's most spectacular temples. Watch the famous Kecak dance at Uluwatu, enjoy fresh seafood in Jimbaran, and witness a magical sunset at Tanah Lot.

Ubud - Tegalalang - Goa Gajah

Cultural Ubud

Explore Bali's cultural heart. Visit ancient temples, walk through stunning rice terraces, and experience traditional Balinese art.

Balian - Medewi - Rambut Siwi

West Coast Adventure

Discover Bali's peaceful west coast. Perfect beaches for surfing, stunning ocean views, and authentic local villages.

Mount Batur

Sunrise Trek

Start early for an unforgettable sunrise from Mount Batur's summit. Includes breakfast with a view and natural hot springs.

North Bali

Waterfall Tour

Chase waterfalls in Bali's lush north. Visit multiple stunning waterfalls, swim in crystal clear pools, and enjoy nature.

Tanah Lot

Temple On The Sea

Embark on a Tanah Lot tour to experience the iconic Hindu temple perched on a rock formation amidst the crashing waves.
